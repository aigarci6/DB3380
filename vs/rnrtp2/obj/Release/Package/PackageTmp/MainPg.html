About us

Pixar Pier was created in 1994, founded by Griffin Pixar. Built in rememberance of Griffin's brother Hank Pixar, who passed away ending of 1992 due to terminal cancer. Griffins commitment after the passing away of his brother was builting his borthers favorite hobby of enjoyment, which was visiting thmeparks. This park symbolizes a everlivng happy childing smiling through the ups and downs of the thrilling rollercoasters.
